About us

As rising sophomores, we have firsthand experience of the rigors of high school academics. As athletes, we have primarily been impacted by a lack of coaching. As teenagers, we can account for the desire for improvement in any area, conflicting the feeling that you will never be good enough because nobody can help you. We want to solve this problem. In our current community,47% of kids find that their external education is insufficient. With Calligo, these kids gain direct access to some o the community's finest tutors, coaches, mentlrs, and so much more. We know how insufficient mentoring feels, and we never want other kids in the community to go through this.
